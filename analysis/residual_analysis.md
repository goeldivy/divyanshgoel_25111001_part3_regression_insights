# Residual Analysis

Residual = Actual Monthly Sales − Predicted Monthly Sales

## Interpretation

- Positive residuals indicate actual sales were higher than predicted.
- Negative residuals indicate actual sales were lower than predicted.

## Business Interpretation

Large positive residuals may represent stores that performed exceptionally well due to factors not included in the model, such as local promotions or seasonal demand.

Large negative residuals may indicate operational issues, unexpected competition, or other external factors affecting sales.

Overall, the model predicts monthly sales reasonably well, but some variation remains unexplained.
