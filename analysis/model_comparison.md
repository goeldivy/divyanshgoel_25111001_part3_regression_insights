# Model Comparison

| Model | Dependent Variable | Independent Variables | R² | Significant Variables | Business Usefulness |
|------|---------------------|-----------------------|------|----------------------|--------------------|
| Simple Regression 1 | Monthly Sales | Marketing Spend | Moderate | Marketing Spend | Shows how marketing impacts sales |
| Simple Regression 2 | Monthly Sales | Footfall | Moderate | Footfall | Shows customer traffic impact |
| Multiple Regression | Monthly Sales | Marketing Spend, Footfall, Avg Discount %, Staff Count, Inventory Availability | High | Marketing Spend, Footfall, Inventory Availability | Best model because it explains sales using multiple business factors |

## Limitations

- Regression shows association, not causation.
- Other external factors may influence sales.
- Some business variables were not included.
