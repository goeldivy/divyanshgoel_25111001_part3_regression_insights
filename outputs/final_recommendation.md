# Final Business Recommendation

The regression analysis shows that marketing spend, footfall, inventory availability, and staffing levels are positively associated with monthly sales.

Management should focus on increasing effective marketing campaigns, improving inventory availability, and maintaining adequate staffing to support customer demand.

The multiple regression model provides better insights than the simple regression models because it considers several business factors simultaneously.

Regression identifies relationships between variables but does not prove that one variable directly causes another. Business decisions should therefore combine regression results with operational knowledge and market conditions.
